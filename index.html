WELCOME TO EMBLEM OF LOVE FOUNDATION

The donation you make you help serve humanity!!!

EMBLEM OF LOVE is an assocaition of teenagers who have come together with the intention of visiting the poor and the widow in their afflictions. The vision was solicited from the charge to us by God in the Bible Scripture "James 1:27".This association was formed in the year 2024.
Our main obligation is to help the poor and needy with our donatative materials. Moving forward, we also spread the gospel through the donation period as it is a criteria under the charge.
